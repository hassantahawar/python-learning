# PART 2 — JOB TITLES & EXPECTATIONS (REALISTIC)

## 🧩 Junior LLM Engineer (0–3 months)

**Expectations**

* Build chat APIs
* Write good prompts
* Handle errors
* Ship features

❌ No model training
❌ No research

---

## 🧩 LLM Engineer (3–6 months)

**Expectations**

* Build RAG systems
* Reduce hallucinations
* Optimize cost
* Design AI flows

✅ This is where most hiring happens

---

## 🧩 Senior LLM Engineer / Applied AI Engineer (6–12 months)

**Expectations**

* Choose models
* Design architectures
* Own reliability
* Mentor juniors

---

## 🧩 AI Engineer (12+ months)

**Expectations**

* Decide build vs buy
* Own AI roadmap
* Ensure safety & ROI
* Explain AI to leadership

❌ Not a researcher
❌ Not a data scientist

---
